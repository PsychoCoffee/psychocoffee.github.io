Introduction

Hello! My name is Luka Beg, and I come from Croatia. I am a student, but in my free time I work with programming and electrical engineering. I use C#, C++ and Python.

In my free time, I like to work in small solo projects and experiments in C++ and C#.

I have 6 years of experience in using the Unity Engine, and I have participated in a few game jams. My current priorities are country-level competitions in software development organized by the Croatian Education Agency (AZOO).

Projects

LGS (Logic Gate Simulator)

LGS was a project for the country-level software development competition in the school year 2023. LGS uses simple user interfaces, and simple options for all kinds of users to understand logic gates: from children, to even engineers!

Screencast video

SLS can be used in all kinds of applications: from schools and education institutions, to engineering offices and even for personal use.

If you are interested in using the SLS software, please contact me using my personal e-mail.
